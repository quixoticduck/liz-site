Go to http://tinymce.moxiecode.com/download.php

Download the latest version of PHP Spellchecker plugin.

Uncompress and copy spellchecker directory to this directory (wysiwyg_spellcheck/tinymce).

You may want to edit wysiwyg_spellcheck/tinymce/spellchecker/config.php file for your choice of spellchecker engine and its settings.
